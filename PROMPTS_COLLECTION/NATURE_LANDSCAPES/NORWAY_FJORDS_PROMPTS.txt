НОРВЕЖСКИЕ ФЬОРДЫ - ПЕЙЗАЖНЫЕ ПРОМПТЫ

=== ДРАМАТИЧНЫЕ НОРВЕЖСКИЕ ФЬОРДЫ ===

Breathtaking Norwegian fjord landscape: deep sapphire blue waters reflecting towering vertical cliffs, emerald green grass covering rolling hills, distant snow-capped peaks, massive white clouds creating natural light patterns across the vast Nordic sky. Golden hour sunset lighting with sun breaking through clouds creating dramatic reflections on fjord water surface. Crystal clear Scandinavian air, dramatic Nordic weather patterns, glacier-carved valleys, vertical cliff faces rising thousands of feet, carpets of vibrant green grass and wildflowers.

=== ОСВЕЩЕНИЕ НОРВЕЖСКИХ ПЕЙЗАЖЕЙ ===

- Золотой час: golden hour sunset lighting, warm orange and pink sky
- Рассвет: soft dawn light, misty fjord atmosphere, cool morning tones  
- Драматичное небо: massive white and grey clouds, natural light patterns
- Отражения: dramatic reflections on water surface, mirror-like fjord waters

=== ВРЕМЕНА ГОДА ===

ЛЕТО: emerald green grass, wildflowers, midnight sun, never-setting sun
ЗИМА: snow-covered peaks, ice formations, aurora borealis, dramatic winter light
ОСЕНЬ: autumn colors, dramatic weather, storm clouds
ВЕСНА: fresh green vegetation, waterfalls, clear mountain air

=== ТЕХНИЧЕСКИЕ НАСТРОЙКИ ===

- Линзы: wide angle 14-24mm, landscape photography
- Диафрагма: f/8-f/11 for maximum sharpness
- Цветопередача: Fujifilm Velvia film stock, enhanced natural saturation
- Композиция: dramatic foreground, leading lines, rule of thirds
